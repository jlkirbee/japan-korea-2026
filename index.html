üí≥ Cash vs Card Tips

üáØüáµ Japan

  • Carry cash: ¬•10,000‚Äì20,000 at all times. Many small restaurants, izakaya, and market stalls are cash-only.
  • IC card (Suica/Pasmo): Load in Apple Wallet before arrival. Works for trains, buses, konbini, vending machines.
